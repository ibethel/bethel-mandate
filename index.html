How to Cancel your PayPal Account

If you already give regularly online through your PayPal account , you can follow these steps to cancel your giving and switch to our new site.

STEP 1

Log into your PayPal account.

STEP 2

Click Profie in the center menu.

STEP 3

Click My Money.

STEP 4

Click Update in the My Preapproved Payments section.

STEP 5

Click Cancel, Cancel Automatic Billing, or Cancel Subscription and follow the instructions.

STEP 6

Go to Give.iBethel.org to make new payments.
